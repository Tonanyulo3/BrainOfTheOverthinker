This is a dodger game
You have to dodge the colores balls with your mouse
You must endure as long as you can

Controls:
- Left click the mouse to stop time (5s cooldown/1s effect)
- Right click the mouse to start on paused game

The point count are positioned on the center of the screen once you lose (miliseconds)

Special Features:
- Za Warudo timestop sound effect
- Made In Heaven time acceleration sound effect (occurs every 20s)
- Roblox Man Face
- RGB shimmering player
- If you die in a sudden manner without having touched any of the enemies, 
  it's part of an unwanted bug I couldn't fix
